Privacy Policy

Muslim Prayer App is built with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

No data collectedNo personal information is ever collected

No adsCompletely ad-free experience

No trackingNo analytics or third-party SDKs

Works offlineAll features work without internet

🚫

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track your location history or usage patterns.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or crash reporters that send data externally.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • The app has no backend server — everything runs entirely on your device.
📍

Location Access

  • Location is requested only to calculate accurate prayer times and the Qibla compass direction.
  • Your location is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to any server.
  • We request When In Use location access — the app never accesses location in the background.
  • You can revoke location access at any time in: iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Muslim Prayer.
  • Without location access, all other features (Quran, Tasbeeh, Duas, etc.) continue to work normally.
💾

On-Device Storage

  • Prayer preferences, Tasbeeh counts, fasting records, and Quran bookmarks are stored locally on your device only.
  • Data is stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults — it never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud Sync.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.
☁️

iCloud Sync (Optional)

  • iCloud Sync is an optional feature you must explicitly enable in the app's Settings.
  • When enabled, data is stored in your own private iCloud account — only you can access it.
  • We never see or access your iCloud data.
  • Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy applies to synced data: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • You can disable iCloud Sync at any time from Settings.
🌐

Internet & Audio Streaming

  • The app works fully offline. Internet access is only used for Quran audio playback.
  • When you tap the play button on an ayah, audio is streamed from everyayah.com — a public, free Quran audio CDN.
  • No personal information is transmitted during audio streaming. Only the surah and ayah number are requested (as part of the audio file URL).
  • Prayer times, Qibla, Tasbeeh, Duas, and all other features are fully offline.
🔔

Notifications

  • Notification permission is requested only to alert you for prayer times and Sunnah reminders.
  • All notifications are scheduled locally on your device — no push notification servers are used.
  • You can manage or disable notifications at any time: iPhone Settings → Notifications → Muslim Prayer.
👶

Children's Privacy

  • This app does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
  • There are no in-app purchases, advertisements, or social features.
  • The app is safe and appropriate for users of all ages (rated 4+ on the App Store).
🔄

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app's features.
  • The latest version is always available at this URL and inside the app under Settings → Privacy Policy.
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the app handles data, feel free to reach out via the App Store.
